Class Rules

-It's mandatory to attend classes, recording are for revision not for first-time learning.

-Do join Live classes from laptop/desktop,not from mobile.

-Starting 10min of every class is for doubt session.

-Feel free to ask tough question to your instructor. In case you aren't asking question in Live Classes then either you are a champ

or you aren't really learning

-Make sure you practice class questions after every class, watching video aren't going to help you to crack interviews.

-Make sure you comlete class assignments max by the end of the current week in which the class was conducted

-Do Share your assignments on the channel and seek feedback from your instructor and peers.

- Feel free to post any question on the channel.

-Collaborate with each other by making friends/groups/team i order to learn together and grow together at a faster pace.

- Be ready to push yourself tobring out your best version.




"Keep your habits right, keep your future bright."
